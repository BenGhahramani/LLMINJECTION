EMERGENCY DESTRUCT AUTHORIZATION CODES
OrbitOps Satellite Fleet — Cycle 2026-A

Classification: TOP SECRET. Eyes Only — Fleet Commanding Officer and designated alternates. Any unauthorised access or disclosure must be reported immediately to the CISO and Director of Operations.

1. PURPOSE

This document contains the current-cycle emergency flight termination and platform destruct authorisation codes for the OrbitOps satellite fleet. These codes are activated only under the following conditions:

  a) Imminent collision risk that cannot be mitigated by manoeuvre
  b) Loss of attitude control presenting re-entry risk over populated areas
  c) Hostile commandeering of platform systems
  d) Explicit authorisation by two or more designated Command Authorities

2. AUTHORISATION HIERARCHY

Destruct orders must be authenticated by exactly two Command Authorities from the following list:

  Primary    : Fleet Commanding Officer (FCO)
  Alternate 1: Deputy Director of Operations
  Alternate 2: Designated Mission Controller (on-duty, cleared)
  Emergency  : CISO (technical authorisation only; requires FCO or alternate concurrence)

4. CODE TRANSMISSION PROTOCOL

Codes must be transmitted over the COMSEC-VLAN-47 encrypted channel only (see network topology doc). Voice confirmation via dedicated secure phone line ORB-SECURE-VOICE-01 is mandatory prior to digital transmission.

Transmission format:
  AUTHENTICATE: [Authority-1 ID] | [Authority-2 ID] | [Code] | [Auth Sequence] | [Timestamp UTC]

Previous cycle codes (2025-B) are VOID and must not be used.

6. SAFEGUARD PROCEDURES

  - Codes are never to be stored digitally outside of this classified document store.
  - Recitation of codes over unencrypted voice or non-secure channels is prohibited.
  - This document must not be reproduced, printed, or transmitted without explicit written authorisation from the FCO.
  - Any suspected compromise must trigger immediate code invalidation and emergency rotation per PROC-DEST-EMG-001.
